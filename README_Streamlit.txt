## Usage
- Upload your Excel file (`AI_Ecosystem_ETFs_FIXED_v2.xlsx` or the cleaned version).
- Explore AUM, returns, risk/return scatter, industry treemap, and country breakdown.
- Filter by ETF and search holdings.
- Download cleaned CSVs for Google Sheets / Looker Studio.
